Our Story

We built the tool we wished existed

StormCloud AI started with a simple frustration: UK small businesses were paying agencies thousands for SEO they couldn't understand, couldn't verify, and couldn't act on themselves.

Our Mission

Giving UK small businesses an unfair advantage

The best SEO tools used to be reserved for businesses with big agency budgets. AI has changed that — and we built StormCloud to put those tools in the hands of every UK small business owner.

Our platform scans your site, scores it, and generates a personalised AI action plan in minutes. No jargon, no waiting — just clear answers and a plan you can actually act on.

Free to start. No agency, no markup, no middleman. Just your site, our scanner, and an AI that tells you exactly what to fix.
