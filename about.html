Our Story

SEO that's honest, measurable and built for SMEs.

StormCloud AI was built because UK small businesses deserve the same quality of SEO and digital marketing as enterprise — without the enterprise price tag, the jargon or the lock-in contracts.

Our Mission

Make SEO simple, transparent and effective.

Most small businesses know SEO matters — but they're either ignored by big agencies or sold packages they don't understand. We built StormCloud to fix that.

Our AI-powered platform runs the same kind of audits that enterprise SEO teams use — broken links, Core Web Vitals, schema, indexation, keyword targeting — but presents the results in plain English with a clear, prioritised roadmap.

You can use the platform yourself, or hand the work to us. Either way, you'll always know what's happening, why it matters, and what to expect.
