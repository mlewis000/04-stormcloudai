Under 3 minutes for most sites. We run 50+ checks in parallel — you'll have your score and full issue list before you've finished making a coffee.

Most fixes don't require any technical knowledge. Every issue comes with plain English instructions. The ones that do need code — we flag them clearly and tell you exactly what to ask a developer to do.
What happens after the free scan?
You get your score, a full breakdown of every issue, and your personalised AI action plan — all free, no card needed. If you want to rescan monthly and track your progress over time, that's the Starter plan at £49/month.
