Common Questions

Before you sign up — quick answers.

Is there a contract?
No. All plans are month-to-month. Cancel any time from your account settings — no notice period, no cancellation fee. Your data stays accessible until the end of your billing period.
Can I upgrade or downgrade later?
Yes. Start free and upgrade when you need more. Downgrade any time. Your full scan history and AI plans carry over — nothing gets lost when you change plans.
What counts as a "client site"?
Any domain you add to your dashboard that isn't your own. You can run as many scans as your plan allows on each client site. Client limits reset on the 1st of each month.
What counts as a "scan"?
One full crawl of a domain — 50+ checks, full report, fresh AI action plan. Scan limits reset on the 1st of each month on paid plans. The free scan never expires, it's just one per account.
Do I need a developer to use StormCloud?
No. Every issue comes with plain English instructions your clients can share with their developer — or often action themselves. For issues that need code, we tell you exactly what to ask for.
What does "white-label" mean on Consultancy?
On the Consultancy plan, PDF reports are produced under your branding rather than StormCloud AI's. Your logo, your colours, your name on the cover. Clients see a professional report from your agency.
